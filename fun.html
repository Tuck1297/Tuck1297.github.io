Aspirations

More than anything, I want to live my life with no regrets. Either it be working, hanging out with family or traveling, I intend to do so with an 'ALL IN' mentality.

Hiking - Traveling - Photography

When I am not hanging out with family, in the classroom, or at work there are three things that I love doing more than anything else. They are hiking, traveling, and photography. The perfect trifecta! Over the last four years I have been fortunate enough to travel, hike and take photos from the platform of Mt. Rushmore to the valleys of Yellowstone National Park. My favorite hike overall would have to be to the top of Black Elk Peak where it feels like the land is touching the sky.
